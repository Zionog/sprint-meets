Nike Air Zoom Maxfly 2
Nike · Most aggressive sprint spike
What it is: Maxfly 2 refines the original with a stiffer ZoomX + carbon plate stack. Among the stiffest sprint spikes available — designed to work as a lever off a surface that pushes back hard.

Best on: Premium, fresh MONDO at elite venues. Stiffer surface = more energy returned through the plate.
✓ Elite MONDO (Hengelo, Eindhoven, Breda, Oordegem)~ Good MONDO (Den Haag, Heusden-Zolder, Wetzlar)✗ Older/softer (Liège, Rotterdam)
Nike Air Zoom Maxfly 1
Nike · Slightly more versatile
What it is: Original Maxfly — full carbon plate + ZoomX, slightly less rigid than Maxfly 2. More forgiving on imperfect surfaces.

Best on: Most decent synthetic tracks. Sweet spot for "good but not elite" venues.
✓ Elite MONDO + good synthetics (Hengelo, Kortrijk, Leuven)~ Mid-tier (Rotterdam)✗ Old/worn (Liège)
Adidas Adizero Prime SP3
Adidas · Most aggressive sprint spike
What it is: Top of Adidas sprint line — full carbon plate, EnergyRods, race-day only. Refined upper vs SP2, same aggressive plate.

Best on: Elite, firm MONDO. Leverages off firm surfaces for massive toe-off.
✓ Elite MONDO (Hengelo, Eindhoven, Oordegem, Wetzlar, Den Haag)~ Good MONDO (Heusden-Zolder, Breda)✗ Soft/older (Liège, Rotterdam)
Adidas Adizero Prime SP2
Adidas · Aggressive, slightly more accessible
What it is: One gen behind SP3 — still high-end carbon plate. Functionally very similar. Often available at better prices.

Best on: Premium and good-tier MONDO. Marginally more usable on competition-level synthetics than SP3.
✓ WACT-level MONDO (Kortrijk, Heusden-Zolder)~ Good synthetics (Leuven, competition tracks)✗ Worn/old surfaces (Liège)
Adidas Adizero Ambition
Adidas · Most versatile sprint spike
What it is: More compliant sprint plate — less aggressive than SP2/SP3, more flexible underfoot. Works across a wider range of surfaces.

Best on: Mid-tier and older synthetic tracks where aggressive carbon plates go dead. Also ideal for training, warm-up meets, or unknown surfaces.
✓ Mid-tier + older surfaces (Liège, Rotterdam, Leuven)~ Any track as training/second-choice spike✗ Elite MONDO (leaving time on table vs SP3/Maxfly 2)
Quick decision rule
Fresh MONDO (Hengelo, Eindhoven, Oordegem, Wetzlar) → Maxfly 2 or SP3
Good MONDO / WACT Bronze (Breda, Den Haag, Heusden-Zolder) → SP2, Maxfly 1, or newer
Normal synthetic (Kortrijk, Leuven, Rotterdam) → Maxfly 1 or SP2
Older / slow surface (Liège) → Ambition only
Don't know the surface? → Ambition. It never actively hurts you. The SP3 on a bad track can.
